06 · PRINCIPLES

Gold usage rules

The single biggest reason premium apps feel premium is restraint with their accent colour. Follow this strictly.

Gold IS allowed on

  • The single primary CTA on a screen
  • Active tab indicator (underline)
  • Active icon in the bottom nav
  • Featured price emphasis on deal cards
  • One premium badge per screen (BEST VALUE, VERIFIED, PREMIUM)

Gold is NOT allowed on

  • Secondary buttons (use outlined navy)
  • Filter chips (use cream/grey)
  • Card backgrounds or borders
  • Status chips (use semantic colours)
  • Section dividers, decorative accents
  • Icons in general
07 · COMPONENTS

Buttons

Primary is gold and rare. Secondary is outlined. Ghost is text-only. One primary per screen, maximum.
